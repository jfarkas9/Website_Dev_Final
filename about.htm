About Us

BubblesRUs is family owned and operated, established in 1990. The BubblesRUs family is made up of many divers who have been in the sport for many decades and love being underwater.

We strive to bring excellence to our customers. Our goal is to create a memorable experience for you and your family or friends. Let us know how we can customize a memorable adventure just for you!
